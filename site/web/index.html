Ogres Tabletop

Free and open-source (AGPL-3.0) virtual tabletop that you and your friends can run in the browser. No sign-ups and no ads. Just launch the app below to get started.

Launch
OR

Features

  • Instantly start preparing your game; no sign-ups or ads
  • Start an online collaborative session for your friends
  • Prepare and manage multiple scenes at once
  • Built-in initiative tracker for streamlined encounters
  • Adjust time of day between day, dusk, and midnight
  • Easy to use for other game systems
  • Polygonal fog tool
  • ... and much more planned!

Limitations

  • Basic lighting system
  • No support for 3D maps, tokens, or dice rolling
  • No embedded rules knowledge
  • No video, audio, or music capabilities
  • No integration with D&DBeyond or other services
  • Not optimized for phones or tablets
